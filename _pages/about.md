# Linag's Research

Welcome to the Liang Research Website from [AG Winkler](https://www.geo.uni-frankfurt.de/59244748/AG_Winkler#a_29c0515d-6b7f6354) at Goethe University Frankfurt.

My research focuses on carbon chemistry at high pressures.

## Research Areas

- Synthesis of Functionalized Diamonds
- Synthesis of molecular carbon structures
- Swift heavy ion irradiation on carbon structures
